Hello! I am Eunice!

I am a Junior at the Singapore University of Technology and Design, majoring in Engineering Product Development (Mechanical Engineering). I am an enthusiastic and compassionate student who has a strong passion for improving the lives of people through design and technology, particularly in the healthcare field.

Design is powerful. I believe that through Design, efficiency and accessibility will be improved. Through my studies in SUTD, I started to grow a strong interest in CADing and find myself enjoying the Design-Thinking process. I've taken up several projects outside the curriculum to further expose myself to industrial standards and pick up new skills. I am also a design ideathon enthusiast as I believe such events provide me with opportunities to apply what I've learned, as well as allow me to pick up new perspectives as I meet and work with different peers.

I am looking to leverage my innovative side to bring a positive impact to others.

Leadership Experience

Vice-President | Makerspace Club

As the Vice-President of the Makerspace Club, I took the lead to ensure the maker's culture remains fun and vibrant.

Together with my team, we plan and lead 6 workshops on average for undergraduates every term, with each receiving at least 70 sign-ups. The type of workshops we hold ranges from electronics-related to craft works like woodworking. We like to explore various kinds of workshops as the maker culture is present in most forms of categories!

As the leader, I also organize and distribute tasks according to each executive member's strengths. The opportunity to work and lead the club together with this fun and wholesome team was my biggest takeaway! Confidence, strategic planning & time-management, and problem-solving are some of the skills I've developed from this role.

You can visit our website to know more about the club and our events.

Secretary | Nanyang Acoustic Movement

As the secretary of the club, I planned biweekly guitar sessions, crafted proposals for school-based events (OpenHouse, CCA day, Celebrational Events), and supported my other EXCO members when they needed help. I also took the initiative to approach the Community Service club in the school to collaborate so that our members can have more opportunities to perform while doing good deeds.
